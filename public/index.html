HOW IT WORKS

As easy as 1, 2, 3 ⚡

No app download. No account needed. Just scan and queue.

01
🏥

Admin creates a queue

Set a name, pin the location on the map, and get a unique QR code instantly.

02
📱

User scans the QR code

Opens in browser — no app needed. Location is verified to confirm they're nearby.

03
📝

Enter name & join

Optionally add email for notifications. Instantly see your position in the queue.

04
🔔

Get notified & relax

Sit anywhere nearby. Get an alert when your turn is approaching — no more hovering.
